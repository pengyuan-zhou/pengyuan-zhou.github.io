Intro

I am a Ph.D. student in Collaborative Networking (CoNe) research group at Department of Computer Science, University of Helsinki, Finland. My Ph.D. studies are being supervised by Prof. Dr. Jussi Kangasharju, co-supervised by IEEE Fellow,Prof. Dr. Pan Hui (Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hong Kong) and Prof. Dr. Yuming Jiang (Norwegian University of Science and Technology, Norway).

My research is funded by the prestigious European Union's Marie-Curie fellowship and I am working under their Initial Training Network (ITN) project CleanSky. I am also part of the Finnish Academy of Sciences funded project Bright Clouds Dark Clouds (BCDC). At University of Helsinki, I am a part of the well known NODES research group which housed the creation of Linux operating system in late 1990's.
