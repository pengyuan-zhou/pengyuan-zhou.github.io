Biography

I'm joining the School of Cyberspace Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology China, as a Research Associate Professor, starting in September 2021. Before that, I was a postdoc researcher working with Prof. Pan Hui at the Department of Computer Science, University of Helsinki, and SyMLab in HKUST. In May 2020, I got my Ph.D. from University of Helsinki supervised by Prof. Jussi Kangasharju in Collaborative Networking Lab. My research focuses on large-scale distributed networking AI systems.
