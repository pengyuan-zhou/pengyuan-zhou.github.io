About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Collaborative Networking (CoNe) group lead by Prof. Jussi Kangasharju at the Department of Computer Science, University of Helsinki. I am also a member of NODES research group. My study topic is about development of edge computing system based on realistic use cases. The key motivation for my research is the current trend of IoT which brings huge amount of demands to the user end.
