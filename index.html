About me

I am a postdoc researcher working with Prof. Pan Hui at the Department of Computer Science, University of Helsinki, Finland. We have extensive collaborations with SyMLab in HKUST. In May 2020, I got my Ph.D. from University of Helsinki supervised by Prof. Jussi Kangasharju in Collaborative Networking Lab. My research focuses on mobile edge computing and communication systems, connected vehicles and mixed reality offloading. The key motivation for my research is the current trend of IoT which generates heavy demands for computational and networking resources on the edge of the network.
