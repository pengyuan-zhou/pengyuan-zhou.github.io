I am a postdoc researcher working with Prof. Pan Hui at the Department of Computer Science, University of Helsinki, and SyMLab in HKUST.
In May 2020, I got my Ph.D. from University of Helsinki supervised by Prof. Jussi Kangasharju in Collaborative Networking Lab.
My research focuses on mobile edge computing and communication systems, connected vehicles and edge AI.
The key motivation is the heavy demand for computational and networking resources on the edge of the network.
