Biography

Peng Yuan Zhou is currently an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of ECE, Aarhus University. He was a Research Associate Professor in USTC from 2021 to 2023. Before that, he was a PostDoc at Dept. of CS, University of Helsinki, where he got his PhD funded by EU Marie Curie ITN. His research interest is to ensure the trustworthiness of AI and XR, especially in (not limited to) distributed setups. He has publications on Ubicomp, Percom, ICPP, WWW, ICDCS, CVPR, VR, MM, IJCAI, etc.
